EveVision

The EVE Online UI you've always wanted.

If you play EVE, you are probably alt+tabbing to web-based tools every single time you play. It's one of the many things that makes EVE different. However, it also ruins the immersion of the beautiful world CCP built and can cause you to miss things.

The era of having to leave EVE to play EVE is over.

EveVision is a EULA-legal way to extend the UI of EVE Online, bringing the tools you love directly into the game client.
